The Evil

I like the idea of the visual evil being kind of creepy, but this might be too over the top obvious. I like the direction but it might need to be more subtle. This isn’t its true form, but a hidden, papered over version to make it more appealing.

But similar to how seeing the evil for what it is reveals the truth, the true uncloaked evil is horrifyingly disturbing.

One arm is sheered, or carved, into a splintered point, with sinew and nerves still hanging against bone. Its bodily form is swollen to overflowing with wriggling, squamous worms.
