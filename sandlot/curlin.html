rules

  • No Spamming. This includes double posts of any kind, or posts with three words or less. Since the ranks have a lot to do with post amounts, anyone found spamming will not be able to move up a rank until they follow the rules, and if the spamming is out of control, the member will be kicked out.
  • No Hazing. This is a friendly guild, open to everybody who meets the reqs. Nobody should be feeling as if they are left out of any guild activities or conversations. Include everybody!
  • No drama. The last thing anybody wants to deal with on neopets is drama. We have enough of that in our real lives, right? So let's keep the message board friendly, and free of any conflicts. If you have a problem with a member and wish to speak to someone about it, contact sorority or rachel.
  • Have fun! This guild is aimed for people who want to chat and have fun while achieving their avatar goals. ^_^.

ranks

These are the different ranks of T.A.G., along with the different requirements for each one.
  • SOME IVY GROWIN - this is the first rank of the guild. no requirements here :)
  • A DANDELION - this is the second member rank. you achieve this rank by having 500+ posts, 200 avatars, and being in the guild for at least 2 weeks.
  • A ROSE BUSH - this is the third member rank. you achieve this rank by having 1,000+ posts, 205+ avatars, and being in the guild for at least 4 weeks.
  • A CUTE LIL BIRD - this is the fourth member rank. you achieve this rank by having 2,000+ posts, 215+ avatars, and being in the guild for at least 7 weeks.
  • SUNSHINE - this is the fifth and final member rank of the guild! you achieve this rank by having 4,000+ posts, 230+ avatars, and being in the guild for 14 weeks.
