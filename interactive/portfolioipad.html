Portfolio App Concept

iPad app

Portfolio is a GUI designed for an iPad app meant to show off a portfolio. It imports from Dropbox, iCloud, or photos stored on the iPad already. Users can reorder or categorize their photos in much the same way apps are moved around on the home screen. Names of projects and descriptions can also be added. Enter display mode and show off projects by pushing the project up from the bottom of the screen with two fingers or a single tap. From there, simply navigate through project images by swiping up or down, or left and right to change project.
