Groundhog Day Badge

Foursquare badge

While working at Red Tettemer + Partners, I got the chance to design the Foursquare badge for 2012's Groundhog Day. The event was sponsored by PA Tourism. To get the badge, you simply had to follow PATourism on Foursquare and shout the word "groundhog." The badge was Foursquare's second most popular. It was also featured on the front page of Creativity Online. Below you'll also see initial sketches and comps.
